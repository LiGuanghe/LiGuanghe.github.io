\<center\> 仰躺荡波畔，\</center\> 

\<center\>余感水映光。\</center\> 

\<center\> 星布缓河流，\</center\> 

\<center\>联空拢四方。\</center\> 

\<center\>忽而数侧翻，\</center\> 

\<center\>一跃立前行。\</center\> 

\<center\>我心高空外，\</center\> 

\<center\>不及待天明。\</center\>
